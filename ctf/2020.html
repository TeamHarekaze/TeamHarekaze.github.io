About Harekaze mini CTF 2020

Harekaze mini CTF is a Capture The Flag (CTF) competition organized by Harekaze for beginner.

In this competition, we will release challenges related to cybersecurity, such as web security, reverse engineering, cryptography, and so on.

Players can earn points by submitting the flag, which can be found by solving the challenges.

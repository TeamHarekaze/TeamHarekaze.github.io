About Harekaze CTF 2018

Harekaze CTF is a Capture The Flag (CTF) competition organized by Harekaze.

In this competition, we will release challenges related to cybersecurity, such as web security, reverse engineering, cryptography, and digital forensics.

Players can earn points by submitting the flag, which can be found by solving the challenges.
